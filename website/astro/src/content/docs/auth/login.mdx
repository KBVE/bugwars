## Why Sign In?

Creating an account and signing in unlocks the full BugWars experience:

### Save Your Progress
Your game progress, settings, and preferences are automatically saved to your account and synced across all your devices.

### Compete Globally
Join the global leaderboards and see how you rank against players from around the world.

### Unlock Achievements
Track your accomplishments and unlock special achievements as you master the game.

### Cross-Device Play
Start playing on your desktop and continue on your laptop or tablet - your progress follows you everywhere.

## Supported Providers

### GitHub
Sign in with your GitHub account for quick and secure authentication. Perfect for developers!

### Discord
Use your Discord account to sign in and connect with the BugWars community.

## Privacy First

We take your privacy seriously:

- We only request basic profile information (name, email, avatar)
- Your authentication data is encrypted and stored securely
- We never share your personal information with third parties
- You can delete your account at any time

## Already Have an Account?

Simply click your preferred sign-in method above. If you've signed in before, you'll be automatically logged into your existing account.
